About Us

Your Legacy, Our Priority.

Who We Are

At Stoneburgh Management, we specialize in acquiring and operating service-based businesses—HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, and Maintenance—with a mission to honor the hard work, sacrifice, and individuality of each company we partner with. Based in Fort Worth, Texas, we currently manage a growing portfolio of companies, all rooted in the trades, and all led with purpose, integrity, and a long-term vision.

If you’re a business owner considering your next chapter, we’re here to help you transition with confidence—whether you’re retiring, changing paths, or simply ready to explore new opportunities. We don’t just buy companies. We carry on legacies.

What We Do

We offer a comprehensive, confidential process for owners looking to sell while protecting their company’s people, culture, and brand:

  • Accurate Business Valuations based on industry-specific benchmarks
  • Targeted Buyer Matching (when not acquiring directly) to ensure mission and leadership fit
  • Discreet, Professional Marketing that safeguards confidentiality
  • Hands-On Deal Structuring & Exit Planning
  • Due Diligence & Post-Close Support that ensures a smooth transition
