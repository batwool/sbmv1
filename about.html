About Us

Your Legacy, Our Priority.

Who We Are

At Stoneburgh Management, we specialize in acquiring and operating service-based businesses—HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, and Maintenance—with a mission to honor the hard work, sacrifice, and individuality of each company we partner with. Based in Fort Worth, Texas, we currently manage a growing portfolio of companies, all rooted in the trades, and all led with purpose, integrity, and a long-term vision.

If you’re a business owner considering your next chapter, we’re here to help you transition with confidence—whether you’re retiring, changing paths, or simply ready to explore new opportunities. We don’t just buy companies. We carry on legacies.

What We Do

We offer a comprehensive, confidential process for owners looking to sell while protecting their company’s people, culture, and brand:

  • Accurate Business Valuations based on industry-specific benchmarks
  • Targeted Buyer Matching (when not acquiring directly) to ensure mission and leadership fit
  • Discreet, Professional Marketing that safeguards confidentiality
  • Hands-On Deal Structuring & Exit Planning
  • Due Diligence & Post-Close Support that ensures a smooth transition

Why Owners Choose Us

We Buy & Operate, Not Just Broker

We actively purchase businesses and operate them—designing, defining, and delivering clear purpose to employees while driving world-class customer satisfaction.

We Respect the Sacrifice

We understand what it takes to build a service business. We value the long hours, risk, and grit it took to grow your company—and we treat that legacy with the respect it deserves.

No Roll-Ups. No Stripping.

Unlike private equity firms looking to consolidate and flip, we preserve the identity and individuality of every business we acquire. Your name, culture, and people matter—and we keep them intact.

We Build for the Future

Our strategy is focused on long-term, sustainable growth. We hire and train the next generation of tradespeople to ensure operational longevity for decades to come. This isn’t a quick flip—it’s a lasting investment.

We Know the Trades

We’ve built and run service companies ourselves. Our team brings real-world operational expertise to every acquisition, adding structure, accountability, and strategic depth to help great companies become exceptional.
