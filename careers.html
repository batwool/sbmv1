Why work at Stoneburgh companies?

The trades industry has a dirty secret: most companies treat skilled workers like interchangeable parts. You bounce from shop to shop every few years, chasing an extra dollar an hour, never building anything lasting. That's the "gypsy career" path—and it's a dead end.

We built Stoneburgh to be different. Our mission is turning blue collar into gold collar—creating careers where skilled tradespeople can build real wealth, not just a paycheck. When you join a Stoneburgh company, you're not just filling a slot. You're building something that lasts.

We're a long-term holding company, not a private equity roll-up. That means we keep the companies we buy, preserve their names and cultures, and invest in the people who make them run. We don't flip businesses—we build careers.

Candidate Program

Our flagship leadership development track. High-potential team members receive mentorship, business training, and hands-on experience preparing them to run—and eventually own—a company. This isn't just management training; it's an ownership path.

AGM Program

The Assistant General Manager program bridges the gap between technical excellence and operational leadership. You'll learn scheduling, pricing, customer relations, and team management while still working in the field. It's the proving ground for future GMs.

Time & Security

We believe rest makes better workers. Our time-off policies are designed to help you recharge, not just check a box.

9 Paid Holidays

New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving (2 days), Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, and one floating holiday of your choice.

PTO Tiers

Year 1-2: 80 hours (2 weeks)

Year 3-5: 120 hours (3 weeks)

Year 6+: 160 hours (4 weeks)

Sick Time

Separate from PTO. Take care of yourself and your family without dipping into vacation time. Because health shouldn't compete with rest.

Roll-over & Sell-back

Unused PTO rolls over to the next year (up to cap). Or cash it out—we offer sell-back options so your unused time doesn't go to waste.
